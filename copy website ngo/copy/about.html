About Ganga Ayurvedic Chikitsalaya

The foundation of Ganga Chikitsalaya was laid by Late Ram Ugra Pandey 79 years ago, my grandfather. Taking inspiration from my grandfather and father, myself and my sister decided to learn Ayurved too.

In Ganga Ayurved we are a team experienced in Panchkarma procedures, Nisargopchar and Yoga addressing health in totality. Today there are a number of lifestyle disorders such as Prameha/Madhumeha, Thryoid disorders, skin disorders such as Psoriasis, Vitiligo and different hair fall disorders that we are working on. Other diseased states such as Kidney stone, Gallbladder stones, Piles, Diabetic wounds, infertility, liver disorders, Benign tumors/ unnatural growths, digestive disorders and infections are also being dealt here. In those diseases which require Shodhan- a procedure ( that helps bring back normal physiological functioning of body) which is inclusive of Panchakarma is done under our care. And those conditions which require only internal medication to restore health, are manufactured by us.
